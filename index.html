Welcome to the Muhokama webring. A collection of personal spaces maintained by people who share certain ideas or approaches to the Internet. You can also retrieve the list of RSS/Atom feeds associated with the webring using the following OPML file.

We're interested in a range of subjects, for example Software Engineering, Functional programming, Type system, OCaml and Small-Web. If you are interested in the different topics proposed or if you find yourself close to the ideas presented by the different members of the ring, don't hesitate to add your own digital space!
